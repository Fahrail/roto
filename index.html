The official rules page for the ROTO Rome Minecraft server. These rules are here to keep war, politics, building, and roleplay fair, clear, and fun for everyone.

General Server Rules

  • Be respectful to other players. Harassment, toxicity, discrimination, and targeted abuse are not allowed.
  • No NSFW builds, messages, paintings, skins, or content.
  • No unrelated advertising, spam, staff impersonation, or player impersonation.
  • No real-life threats, doxxing, DDoS threats, terrorism promotion, genocide promotion, or extremist content.
  • No harmful lying, fake evidence, or false reports.
  • Loopholes are not a valid defense. Staff may punish behavior that clearly breaks the spirit of the rules.

Housing Requirement

  • Capitals and settlements must have at least 8 houses inside the walls.
  • Each house must be at least 8×8×6 and include a bed, storage, décor, and basic utilities.
  • Housing must look like a realistic dwelling.
  • Settlements must meet housing requirements before unlocking specialized structures.
Housing Rewards: +1 Villager Egg per house per day, and +1 Food per hour.

Military Structures

Military structures require at least a 2x capital requirement unless staff says otherwise.

Barracks

Must include sleeping quarters, training grounds, firing range, stables, and armoury.

Reward: +2 permanent unit capacity, +1 Iron Ingot/hour

Walls

Must fully enclose the settlement with 1 main gate, 1 side gate, and 4 towers.

Reward: +3 Stone Bricks/hour

Gate

Minimum 3 blocks wide with mounted-unit clearance.

Reward: +1 Iron Ingot/hour

Moat

Must allow safe ally crossing. Water and traps are allowed. TNT, fire, and lava are not allowed.

Reward: +2 Clay/hour

No obsidian, waterlogging, or regenerating walls.

War & PvP Rules

War Declarations

  • You must control at least one eligible city or capital to declare war.
  • War declarations against active nations must be posted in the warfare channel.
  • Diplomatic posts must clearly show marching paths, route permissions, and total travel cost.

War Travel

  • Land movement costs 3000 coins per tile.
  • You may only march through your own tiles or allied tiles.
  • Seafaring costs the same as land movement.
  • Seas cannot be blockaded.
  • If opposing forces meet on the same sea path, a naval battle occurs and the winner keeps the route.
Example: A1 → A2 → A3 → B3 costs 12,000 coins.

AI & Inactive Tile Conquering

  • A diplomacy post is required and travel costs still apply.
  • You must border the tile being conquered.
  • Staff will organize the mock siege.

PvP & Combat Rules

  • PvP is always enabled.
  • No handcuffs or trapping items may be used in PvP or war.
  • If a player hides in their base and refuses to fight, you must leave with /stuck after a maximum of 15 minutes.
  • You may not stalk a player and repeatedly attack them.
  • Attacking someone in their territory is a valid casus belli.
  • If you are attacked and go to war over it, travel costs are nullified for one battle.
  • No trapping or blocking bases unless it is self-inflicted.
  • Explosive siege weapons, including battering rams, are banned outside wars.
  • No AFK kills.
  • No teleporting during combat, raids, sieges, hunts, or wars.
  • No combat logging.
  • No dual-wielding combat gear for extra stats.
  • Units cannot be killed outside of wars unless they attack first. Breaking this rule may result in an instant ban.
  • Greek Fire may only be used defensively to protect your own land.

Gear Restrictions

  • No Netherite weapons or armor.
  • No diamond weapons.
  • No vanilla armor, shields, or axes.
  • No enchantments on combat gear. Tool enchants and utility enchants are allowed.
  • No vanilla potions.
  • Potions are capped at Level 1.
  • No Elytra or Ender Pearls for PvP.

Unit Rules

  • Players may have a maximum of 8 units outside of wars.
  • Faction leaders may design custom troop trees for their faction.
  • During wars, factions have a faction-wide limit of 250 troops.
  • Faction leaders may decide how troops are allocated between players.

Relics

  • Relics include holy books and event artifacts.
  • Relics must always be assigned to a town.
  • Relics cannot be transferred to another town during wartime.
  • No town may hold more than 3 relics.
  • If a tile is conquered, the attackers gain the relics assigned to that town.
  • After 30 days of inactivity, relics may reset or be hidden by staff.

Factions & Claiming

  • No sabotage, griefing, or stealing.
  • Espionage, spying, and information-sharing are allowed.
  • Faction leaders must return the items of kicked players.
  • Claims must be within the faction tilemap.
  • Wanderers may claim small areas anywhere.
  • No snake claims, hollow claims, or distant ocean-only claims.
  • Sea claims may only extend up to 4 chunks from the coastline.

Fair Play & Anti-Cheating

  • No hacked clients, autoclickers, macros, alts, or stolen accounts.
  • No teleport traps or combat logging.
  • No griefing outside active sieges.
  • Warps and travel points must stay open.
  • Allowed mods include Optifine, JEI, and minimaps.
  • Report exploits immediately. Valid exploit reports may be rewarded.

Immersive Paintings

  • No profanity, offensive text, slurs, hate speech, or extremist content.
  • No nudity, sexual content, or anything sexual in nature.
  • No graphic, gory, violent, or disturbing content.
  • No references to real-world dictators, terrorists, or figures associated with atrocities.
  • Historical or religious Roman/medieval artwork is allowed only when used respectfully and in context.
